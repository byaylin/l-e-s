House Soft Washing

House Soft Washing in the PNW

Over time, if we don’t wash our clothes, they get dirty, stained, and don’t look their best. The same thing happens to our houses! Dirt, mold, algae, and grime build up on the exterior, making a home look older and less appealing. Now, imagine if we used really harsh scrubbing or strong chemicals on our clothes—eventually, they’d wear out or get damaged. That’s why we use gentle cleaning methods to keep them looking nice. The same idea applies to house washing! Here at Loony Exteriors, we specialize in soft washing, which is like a gentle but powerful bath for your home. Instead of using high-pressure water, which can strip paint and damage siding, we use low-pressure water with our cleaning solutions to safely remove dirt, mold, and mildew without harming your home. This method works great on all types of surfaces—whether you have stucco, wood, vinyl, brick, or Hardie board—and leaves your home looking fresh and clean. Taking care of your home’s exterior is just like taking care of anything valuable—it helps it last longer and look its best. So, just like you wouldn’t wait too long to wash your favorite shirt, don’t wait too long to give your house the cleaning it deserves! Selecting an experienced company is crucial to avoid damaging your siding and surrounding landscape. With years of expertise, Loony Exterios ensures your home is treated with the utmost care. Contact us today for a free siding washing evaluation.

Our Softwash Process

  • 360 inspection: We assess the condition of your home for the best cleaning solution method.
  • Preperation: We protect any surroundings like plants, furniture and other items.
  • Application: Using our low-presssure cleaning solution removing all organic contaminets
  • Final Inspection: We rinse the entire property leaving your home clean, as well as a walkthrough to guarantee your satisfaction.
