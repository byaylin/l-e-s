Roof Cleaning in the PNW

Regular Roof Cleaning Matters

Alright, class, let’s talk about the importance of caring for your roof, especially here in the Pacific Northwest. Many people don’t realize that moss and algae can actually damage their roofs. The moisture in our air causes these organisms grow quickly. If they’re not removed, they can trap water, weaken shingles, and even cause leaks leading to expensive repairs! That’s why regular roof cleaning is essential. Using a soft-wash method, we can safely remove moss and algae without harming the shingles, helping roofs last longer and preventing costly damage. Gloeocapsa magma is the cause of black streaks on roofs and surfaces. Other forms of microorganisms can be found in union with bacteria, including mold, fungi, algae, mildew, lichen, and moss. All these organisms thrive in the warm, moist environment created by a roof. Untreated moss on a roof is not only unsightly but also damages shingles, traps moisture, and shortens the roof’s lifespan. It can lead to leaks, mold growth, and shingle lifting. The same bacteria found on roofs can also grow on wood, concrete, siding, and other surfaces. The good news is that through soft washing, these stains and microorganisms can be safely removed. Here at Loony Exteriors, we believe that treating a roof or surface is essential in eliminating the source of staining rather than just blasting away at the surface.

Our Process

All roofs in the PNW are different — some have more organisms than others. Many factors, such as location and surroundings, play a role. No matter the circumstances, we get the job done. We begin with:

  • A 360° walkthrough inspection of the property.
  • Inspecting the roof for damage, including pipe covers, vents, and other roof components.
  • Ensuring fall protection is adequate for the roof pitch
