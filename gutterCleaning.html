Gutter and Downsprout Cleaning

Your gutters are essential for protecting your home from water damage, but they can easily get clogged with leaves, dirt, and moss—especially in the Pacific Northwest, where we have so many trees. When gutters fill up, they can’t drain properly, leading to overflowing water, roof leaks, foundation damage, and even pest problems. The last thing you want is to be caught off guard by overflowing gutters when the rainy season starts. That’s why regular gutter cleaning is so important. Leaves, needles, and branches can quickly block gutters and downspouts, causing water to spill over onto walkways, erode landscaping, and potentially harm your home’s structure. Keeping your gutters clear ensures proper drainage and helps prevent costly repairs down the road.

Our Process

  • Gutter Inspection & Debris Removal – We check leaky corners or gutter semas. for clogs, remove leaves, pine needles, dirt, and moss, and ensure all channels are clear.
  • Downspout Flushing – We flush downspouts with water to clear blockages and ensure proper drainageon entire perimeter of the property.
  • Final Cleanup & Disposal – All gutter debris is removed from the property, leaving your home clean and shiny.
